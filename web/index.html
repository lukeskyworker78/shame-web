This web is dedicated to expose the shameful policy of CELLS Management towards its workers and their representatives.

CELLS as an institution and its head of Finance and HHRR as an individual, are both suing 5 ALBA workers' representatives in relation to their decision of canceling an assembly in which the head of HHRR was present and refused to leave. Note that said assembly was called for discussing the worker's position in front of a regulation that had been drafted and negotiated in name of CELLS by the head of HHRR herself.

The Management pretends to present this as "supporting a worker who is being discriminated by the Workers' Committee" when in reality, the decision of canceling the meeting was taken by request of other workers who rightly felt that they would not be able to express freely if the counter-part in the negotiations was present in the assembly.

It is also worth noting that CELLS Management has been informed several times in the past of violations of workers' rights. In those occasions they either did nothing, or sided with the abuser (who typically was part of Management or close to it).

In contrast, CELLS is now wasting ALBA's (public) money to sue us and even demand us to pay 6251 €. Therefore, we can only interpret the current case as an excuse to punish us and intimidate the rest of ALBA workers. Of course, we have to pay for our lawyer with our own money which is already a form of punishment independently of the result of the trial.

But where they have got money and company lawyers, we have union and mutual support. Please consider showing your support to your colleagues by printing and posting in visible places one of the posters. Also feel free to share this page with any friends or colleagues at other institutions.
